Hack. Learn. Share.

Side projects are:

  • the the things you do to learn more about how computers work
  • experiments that may someday become viable projects
  • a great excuse to hang out with other geeks

What is sideprojects.org?

  • a group of geeks who get together to talk about and work on projects
  • a reminder to work on your projects
  • an excuse to drink beer and hack code

What are the rules at a meetup:

Do:

  • bring something to work on
  • ask for help with things you're stuck on
  • show off your progress

Don't:

  • bitch about your day job
  • disclose trade secrets or patentable ip

When is the next meetup?

Meetups are held on the 3rd Sunday of each month.

Check our calendar for the next time and location.
